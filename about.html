About Me

Hello! I’m Dibyanshu Mehta, an enthusiastic and dedicated web developer with a passion for building clean, efficient, and user-centric websites.

I recently completed my Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA), where I gained strong foundational knowledge in programming, web technologies, and software development principles.

My journey in web development started with static pages and quickly grew to building dynamic, interactive applications. I’m skilled in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Firebase, and version control with Git & GitHub.

I love learning by doing. My personal projects include a portfolio site, calculator, and real-time chat app. These experiences strengthened both my technical and creative problem-solving abilities.

I’m also a great communicator, fast learner, and passionate about contributing to real-world applications. My goal is to work with a forward-thinking team where I can grow and deliver impactful solutions.
